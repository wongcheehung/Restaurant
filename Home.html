Labuan Fisherman Harbour Restaurant

Labuan Fisherman Harbour Restaurant is a Chinese Restaurant that established in May 2009. It is located inside a building called Paragon Mall, which is opposite to Sharetea, selling various kind of dishes such as Butter Crispy Prawn,Black Pepper Beef,Fried Silver Fish,Salted Egg Soft Shell Crabs, Sabah Vegetables With Egg and Toufu With Minced Meat.
